Who Are We?

Diversify Stock Prediction is an initiative to put the power of financial literacy into the hands of low-income or underrepresnted groups. As many marginalized communities are not given the resources or education on making wise investments, our app is designed to address that disparity. This website is full of educational resources to give anyone a basic understading on the stock market, investments, and equity. We also utilize cutting-edge machine learning technology to help you comprehend current trends on the NYSE.
